一副扑克牌，十五道关卡，三十张规则牌——你的策略，决定一切。

**斗破乾坤**是一款以斗地主规则为核心的Roguelike卡牌冒险游戏。不需要联网，不需要等人，一个人就能体验斗地主的策略深度。

---

**🎴 熟悉的规则，全新的玩法**
单张、对子、顺子、炸弹……你熟悉的斗地主牌型，在这里变成了得分武器。每一手牌的选择都至关重要——选对牌型，打出连击，触发规则牌效果，突破层层关卡。

**🃏 30张独特规则牌**
火中求财、妙手回春、暗度陈仓……每张规则牌都会改变你的策略。构建属于你的牌组流派——专注炸弹的爆破流、追求顺子的连锁流、或是以小博大的逆袭流。

**🏔️ 15层冒险 + Boss挑战**
从乡野牌局出发，一路闯过县城擂台、地主庄园、皇城对弈。每三层一个补给商店，每个Boss都有独特的战斗规则——禁牌令、贪婪税、背水一战……

**🔥 飞升系统 (10级)**
通关后解锁飞升挑战。每一级都让目标分数更高、出牌次数更少。你能登顶第十级吗？

**📅 每日挑战**
每天一局特殊规则挑战。和全球玩家在相同条件下比拼策略。

### 英文描述

One deck. Fifteen floors. Thirty Joker cards. Your strategy decides everything.

**Card Rogue: Dou Po** is a Roguelike card adventure built on Doudizhu (Chinese poker) rules. No internet needed, no waiting for players — experience the strategic depth of Doudizhu as a single-player journey.

---

**🎴 Familiar Rules, Fresh Gameplay**
Singles, pairs, straights, bombs — the classic Doudizhu patterns become your scoring weapons. Every hand matters: pick the right pattern, chain combos, trigger Joker effects, and break through floor after floor.

**🃏 30 Unique Joker Cards**
Each Joker fundamentally changes your strategy. Build your playstyle — an explosive bomb build, a chaining straight build, or a comeback underdog build.

**🏔️ 15 Floors + Boss Battles**
Start from humble village games, fight through county arenas and landlord mansions to the Imperial Court. Supply shops every 3 floors. Each Boss has unique rules — pattern bans, greed tax, no retreat...

**🔥 Ascension System (10 Levels)**
Beat the game to unlock Ascension. Higher targets, fewer plays. Can you reach Level 10?

**📅 Daily Challenge**
One special-rules run per day. Compete with global players under identical conditions.

---

• Fully offline — no internet required
• 15-30 minute runs, pause anytime
• Premium (buy once) — no consumable IAP
• English & Chinese supported
