### 详细描述

**The card game 1.4 billion people play — now as a Roguelike.**

Dou Po Qian Kun takes China's national card game *Dou Di Zhu* (斗地主, literally "Fight the Landlord") and reimagines it as a single-player deck-building roguelike. Think Balatro meets Chinese poker culture.

**How it works:**
• You get 10 cards each floor and limited plays/discards
• Form valid Dou Di Zhu patterns: pairs, straights, bombs, rockets, airplanes...
• Each pattern earns points. Hit the target score to advance.
• Between floors, buy Jokers and Buffs to bend the rules

**60+ Jokers that change the game:**
• 🔥 "Chain Reaction" — doubles combo bonus rate
• 💣 "Scorched Earth" — bombs and rockets deal 2x
• 🎯 "First Strike" — your first play each floor deals 2.5x
• ⚡ "Last Stand" — 3x multiplier on your final play
• ...and 56+ more across common, rare, and legendary tiers

**9 Starting Builds:**
Choose your strategy before each run — explosive, combo, straight, plane, pair, balanced, lucky, master, or gambler.

**Features:**
- 12 authentic Dou Di Zhu patterns with strategic depth
- 15 floors across 3 chapters with Boss battles
- Ascension levels 0-10 for ultimate replayability
- Daily challenges with 7 rotating modifiers
- 17 achievements to unlock
- 8 languages supported
- Haptic feedback + dynamic visual effects
- Newcomer tutorial built in
- No ads, no gacha, no energy systems — one purchase, everything unlocked
